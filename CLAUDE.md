# Project

## Self-Improvement Protocol

After completing significant tasks, review your work:

1. **Check for learnings**: Did you encounter pitfalls, discover workflows, or make important decisions?
2. **Save knowledge**: Use the self-improvement-review skill to document learnings
3. **Avoid repeats**: Before starting complex tasks, check `.claude/knowledge/` for relevant past experiences

Knowledge files:
- `.claude/knowledge/pitfalls.md` — debugging discoveries, common mistakes
- `.claude/knowledge/workflows.md` — proven procedures
- `.claude/knowledge/decisions.md` — architecture choices
